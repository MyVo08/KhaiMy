ABOUT ME

My name is Vo Dinh Khai My, an ambitious and energetic 18-year-old who enjoys learning, taking on new challenges, and creating meaningful impact. I believe every child deserves the opportunity not only to learn, but also to create, contribute, and shape their own future. My aspiration is to build a social enterprise that empowers visually impaired children to become storytellers, contributors, and cultural ambassadors through inclusive learning experiences.

WHAT DEFINES ME

Ambitious Learner

I believe meaningful impact begins with strong foundations. Whether in academics, competitive chess, or research, I constantly challenge myself to pursue excellence so I can create greater opportunities for others.

Purposeful Leader

I lead by building communities, empowering others, and creating opportunities for everyone to grow together.

Social Contributor

I create opportunities for students and visually impaired children to learn, connect, and grow through chess, board games, and inclusive learning experiences.
